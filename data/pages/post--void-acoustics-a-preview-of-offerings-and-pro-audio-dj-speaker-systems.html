Void Acoustics: A Preview of Offerings and Pro Audio DJ Speaker Systems

Posted on 
July 5, 2023

Fresh off a successful display at the Integrated Systems Europe (ISE) event in Barcelona..

The forthcoming event promises an exciting array of key flagship products, alongside a selection of major new additions to the brand's pro-audio catalogue.

‍Void Acoustics is gearing up for another impactful presentation.

New Product Showcases at InfoComm

On the roster for InfoComm are innovative products that not only add new dimensions to Void Acoustics' existing offerings but also cater to the diverse needs of several industry sectors. These new products are the Air 15, an IP-rated, reflex-loaded 15” fibreglass subwoofer; the Cyclone 208, a powerful yet discreet IP-rated double 8” subwoofer; the Inca 500, a space-saving and energy-efficient integration amplifier; and the Arclite, a high-powered, arrayable point source loudspeaker.

These new entrants provide sound solutions across sectors served by Void Acoustics, from commercial and outdoor applications to high-end residential projects and the touring industry. Each product is a testament to Void Acoustics' commitment to producing superior audio equipment that resonates with the requirements of diverse settings and user bases.

‍

Pro-Audio DJ Systems for Homes or Studios

As more people invest in creating personal sound spaces, be it at home or in the studio, the demand for high-quality, professional audio DJ systems is growing. Void Acoustics understands this need and offers a range of products to help create the perfect sound environment, whatever your budget may be.

‍

Three Different System Packages

  1. System 1 - Cyclone 55 system, powered with Venu 210i V2: This entry point system, typically priced around £4,000 - £5,000 GBP or $4,500 - $5,500 USD, includes two Cyclone 55 loudspeakers and a powered Venu 210i V2 subwoofer. This compact system saves space without compromising on sound quality, offering an impressive frequency response of 40 Hz - 23 kHz ±3 dB.
  2. System 2 - Air 8, Venu 112 V2 & Bias Q1: This system, aimed at those with a slightly higher budget (£5,000 -£6,000 GPB or $6,500 - $7,500 USD), offers two Air 8 loudspeakers, one Venu 112 V2 subwoofer, and one Bias Q1 amplifier. With a frequency response of 40 Hz - 20 kHz ±3 dB, this system is perfect for those who desire a home-DJ or studio setup.
  3. System 3 – Airten V3, Venu 115 V2 & Bias Q2 System: This premier system, priced in the region of £11,000 - £12,000 GBP or $12,000 - $13,000 USD, boasts a pair of celebrated Airten V3 loudspeakers, a Venu 115 V2 subwoofer, and a Bias Q2 system. The system, noted for its presence in significant venues worldwide, offers an expansive frequency response of 38 Hz - 20 kHz ±3 dB.

‍

‍

Conclusion

Void Acoustics' strength lies in its dedication to providing high-quality sound solutions to cater to varying needs, evident in its new product lineup for InfoComm and its diverse range of pro-audio DJ systems. With their innovative design, premium sound quality, and flexible pricing,Void Acoustics is transforming the way we experience sound at home, in studios, and at commercial venues.

Each of the system packages discussed offers unique sound features, designed with specific usage scenarios in mind. But regardless of the choice, each system guarantees the unparalleled audio quality that Void Acoustics is known for.

To get the most out of your audio experience, consider your unique needs and requirements. And remember, these product package recommendations are not set in stone and can be adapted to your specific needs.

If you're interested in getting a quote for any of the systems outlined in this article, simply fill out our contact page. Embark on your sonic journey with Void Acoustics - it's an experience that truly resonates.

Void enjoys ISE 2023

Void Acoustics has reported an ‘exceptional’ response to their notable presence at the recent ISE trade show in Barcelona.

The company returned to ISE by way of kick starting its 20th anniversary year celebrations.  And the team were thrilled with the response to the six major products they launched to the show’s global audience. These included a new fibreglass subwoofer for the installation market, the Air 15; the powered Venu 208i subwoofer for small and medium sized venues; the stand-alone amplifier the Inca 500; and the latest Bias amplifier – the Bias Q1.5.  

Of particular note were several major orders taken throughout the show, most significantly for the brand new arrayable point source loudspeaker, the Arclite for the touring sector, and the latest addition to the IP55 rated Cyclone series – the Cyclone 208 for commercial and residential projects.

‍

‍

ISE boasts an impressive international audience and the team were delighted to meet a real mix of nationalities from Europe, North & South America, the Middle East, India and beyond. There were even opportunities to explore new partnerships to further enhance Void’s global dealer network and expand the brand into new regions.  

Founders, Alex Skan and Rog Mogale were on hand throughout the show to meet current customers and potential new partners, with Rog delivering daily sessions in Void’s demo room. Indeed across the four-day show, over 1,500 show visitors attended the full-on Void experience in the demo room, as each session was packed out and smiling listeners left the room having been impressed by what they heard. Demos were delivered in English and French to ensure as many people as possible benefitted from the in-depth information the company was sharing.

“We were determined to have a ‘wow’ presence at the show this year, and I don’t think we disappointed with our iconic Incubus Air Array taking centre-stage on our stand”, smiles Dan Moors, Head of Sales & Marketing. “We received an overwhelmingly positive response across the board: to the stand design; our extensive product catalogue and the addition of our six new products to the portfolio; and the Void Acoustics brand in general.”

“We are still only in Q1 for the year, but we can see that 2023 is already successful, and so our 2024 plans are well underway to build on this positive vibe and further develop our success in conjunction with our important partner, dealer and distributor global network.”
