Energizing Control: Savant’s Leap into the Future of Smart Home Automation

In a world where technological advancements are constantly redefining our everyday lives, Savant Systems LLC stands at the forefront of innovation in the home automation industry.

As a certified dealer and installer of Savant products in the Coachella Valley, AVIR is excited to delve into Savant’s recent announcements that mark a significant stride towards integrating audio, energy consumption control, and lighting into the smart home ecosystem. This story explores Savant’s latest offerings and their implications for a more connected and efficient future.

‍

‍

Savant’s Smart Power Box Family: A New Era in Energy Management

The introduction of Savant’s Smart Power Distribution Units (PDU) and Smart Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) is a game-changer in energy management. These products blend the convenience of smart home functionality with advanced power control. The Savant Power Box 8 and Power Box 3, with their individual addressable outlets and built-in surge protection, not only offer comprehensive energy monitoring but also enhance homeowner insights. Set to be available in early 2024, these Smart PDUs promise a future where energy efficiency is seamlessly integrated into our daily lives.

‍

‍

Siri Integrations: Elevating Voice Control in the Savant Ecosystem

Savant’s integration with Apple’s Siri represents a significant leap in voice-controlled automation. This collaboration allows users to enjoy natural language voice recognition and control over their entire home environment. From triggering Savant Scenes to controlling the Cync platform, Siri integration brings a new dimension of convenience and accessibility to the Savant system. This move not only bridges the gap between DIY and professional smart home setups but also opens doors to a more intuitive and connected living experience.

‍

‍

A Smart Home Starts with Smart Power

Understanding and controlling energy usage is the backbone of a true smart home. Savant’s powerful energy management tools, accessible through its user-friendly app, provide real-time and historical insights into energy consumption. With features like Energy Modes, Circuit Control, and Storm Watch, homeowners can proactively manage their energy use, ensuring efficiency and preparedness for any situation.

‍

‍

Filling Homes with Music: The Evolution of Audio Experience

Savant’s advancements in audio technology redefine the way we interact with music at home. The enhanced Savant Music experience, with its personalized dashboard and the ability to create playlists across multiple services, offers a unique, immersive audio experience. Integration with services like Apple Music and Spotify Connect further enriches this experience, making Savant a leader in providing professional-quality audio in personalized settings.

‍

‍

The Savant Power System: Redefining Smart Energy Management

The Savant Power System exemplifies the future of smart energy management. From monitoring and controlling circuits at the distribution panel to managing backup power sources, this system ensures that every aspect of home energy is smart and efficient. The integration of components like Savant Power Modules, Hybrid Inverter, and Level 2 EV Charger, managed by Savant’s advanced software, delivers an unparalleled energy management experience.
